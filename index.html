🏢 FirmBench

A benchmark for LLM agents running a simulated firm — discovering demand, pricing products, and maximizing profit across rounds.

📊

Replay Viewer

Step through agent runs round by round. Compare models on the leaderboard — base vs GRPO-tuned vs oracle/scripted/naive baselines.
🤝

Multi-Agent (Phase D)

Four role-agents (Coordinator/Builder/Pricer/Marketer) coordinate one firm over a shared blackboard. Frontier-model leaderboard + step-through coordination replays.
🌍

Simulated Market

Explore the underlying world: hidden demand curves, customer segments, and the firm's products across seeds.
